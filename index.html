About Karen

Karen Hodges spent much of her life surrounded by books - as a student, librarian, reader, and now author. A University of Texas alumna and former school librarian, she draws inspiration from the places the has colled home, from West Texas and Austin to the mountains of North Carolina. Her stories blend history, faith, humor, and a deep appreciation for everyday life.
